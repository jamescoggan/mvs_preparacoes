# Website Transformation Brief

## Business Context

MVS Preparacoes is an automotive performance preparation workshop in Sao Paulo, Brazil. Existing site content states that the company officially opened in early 2008 to meet demand for motor customization, electronic injection, and peripherals, and that founder Marcelo began studying and working with personalized high-performance automotive projects in 2006.

## Audience

- Primary: car owners and enthusiasts in Sao Paulo evaluating a personalized high-performance project for street or track use.
- Secondary: returning followers from social channels who need location, hours, phone, email, or a clearer project-intake path.

## Conversion Goals

- Primary: start a project conversation through WhatsApp, phone, or email.
- Secondary: help visitors understand service fit before contacting MVS and keep official social channels reachable without distracting from contact.

## Scope

Transform the single-page Jekyll site, layout, styling, metadata, contact flow, build configuration, and deployment readiness. No DNS, GitHub Pages settings, repository secrets, or external accounts are changed.

## Assumptions

- The mobile number can be used as the preferred WhatsApp contact because the official Instagram search snippet mentions budgets by email and WhatsApp, and the current site already publishes the mobile number.
- The site is deployed by GitHub Pages from the repository source with Jekyll-compatible static output.
- No real project photography, customer testimonials, horsepower figures, certifications, awards, or partner logos are available in the repository, so those claims are not invented.

## Success Criteria

- First screen communicates who MVS is, what it provides, where it operates, and how to start contact.
- No placeholder content remains.
- Unsupported claims are removed or softened.
- Mobile navigation works.
- Contact path is clearer than the previous mailto-only form.
- Production build succeeds.
- SEO metadata, language, favicons, and custom-domain files are coherent.
- Browser QA covers desktop and mobile responsive behavior, keyboard navigation, reduced motion, and console/network errors.
